# Self Refinement

## Model-Level Concepts

- `Problem`
- `AssumptionSet`
- `FormalSpec`
- `VerificationEvidence`
- `RefinementPlan`
- `TraceabilityMap`

## Repository-Level Realization

- `problem.md` realizes `Problem`
- `assumptions.yaml` realizes `AssumptionSet`
- `spec.tla` and `spec.cfg` realize `FormalSpec`
- `verification.json` realizes `VerificationEvidence`
- `refinement.md` realizes `RefinementPlan`
- `traceability.json` realizes `TraceabilityMap`

The repository structure itself is the refinement of the methodology into operational artifacts.
